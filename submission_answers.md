## Algorithm I implemented
I ended up going with the `Fisher-Yates Shuffle` algorithm as it's relatively simple to understand. I have an intuition of how it works in that it always guarantees a non-repeating 4 digit sequence, and I can easily explain it to someone non-technical. I also appreciate that every digit between 0 and 9 has an equal change to be selected!

## One thing AI helped with
AI was instrumental in first planning out what we needed to do! I had to guide it, but I love haveng back-and-forth sessions where we determine the best approach to implementing the app.

## One thing I had to correct
AI is not great at frontend out of the box. I brought in a `frontend-design` skill to help make the app feel more original and not just AI-generated. For example, I had to use it to remove the use of purple gradients, as well as telling it to make the guess results in a scrollable element so it didn't just take over the whole screen.
